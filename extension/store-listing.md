## English Description

**Clippy is back — and this time he's on your side.**

Ever wondered why your YouTube feed keeps showing the same type of content? Why your Facebook is full of political posts? Why TikTok won't stop recommending the same genre?

The Algorithm That Repented (האלגוריתם שחזר בתשובה) is a browser extension that analyzes your social media browsing and gives YOU control.

**Features:**
- 📊 Auto-categorizes content you see on social media
- 🔍 Explains WHY you're seeing what you're seeing
- 🗑️ Auto-removes content you don't want (learns your preferences)
- ❤️ Learns from your feedback over time
- 📈 Shows transparent browsing statistics

**Supported sites:** X/Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, TikTok

**Privacy first:** All data stays on your device. No servers. No tracking. No data selling.

**Interface:** Hebrew (RTL). English version coming soon.
