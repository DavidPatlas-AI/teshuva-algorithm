# How to Record a Demo GIF for Twitter

## What to show (30–60 sec)

1. Open YouTube / X feed in browser
2. Show Clippy appearing in corner
3. Hover over a post → show 👍/👎 buttons floating
4. Press 👎 → Clippy animates + "Not interested" auto-clicked on the real post
5. Open Clippy panel → show category weights updating in real time

## Recording tools

- **ScreenToGif** (free, Windows) — records a region as .gif
- **ShareX** (free) — records mp4, convert to gif with Gifski
- Recommended resolution: 1280×720, 15fps, max 15MB

## Upload

- Twitter accepts GIF up to 15MB / 60 sec
- Attach to the tweet in TWEET.md
- Pin the tweet to your profile
